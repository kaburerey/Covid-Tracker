What is COVID-19 ?

COVID-19 is a mild to severe respiratory illness caused by a coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2. A coronavirus is simply a group of RNA viruses that cause disease in humans and animals. Researchers are still racing to make sense of this viral recipe, which could inspire drugs, vaccines and other tools to fight the ongoing pandemic.

What are symptoms ?

Symptoms can range from mild to severe illness, and appear 2-14 days after you are exposed to the virus that causes COVID-19. The most prevalent symptoms are: trouble breathing, persistent pain or pressure in the chest, new confusion, inability to wake or stay awake, and bluish lips. In addition, those with a weak immune system or immune-mediated inflammatory diseases are even more prone to Covid-19 infection and need to take extra safety measures.
